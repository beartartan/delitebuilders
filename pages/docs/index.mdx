## Welcome to Delite ü•Å

Delite is a decentralized, Ethereum-equivalent ZK-Rollup ([Type 1 ZK-EVM](https://mirror.xyz/labs.delite.eth/w7NSKDeKfJoEy0p89I9feixKfdK-20JgWF9HZzxfeBo)). We're working on the full Ethereum [ZK-EVM circuits](https://github.com/privacy-scaling-explorations/zkevm-circuits) as part of a community effort led by the Ethereum Foundation's Privacy and Scaling Explorations (PSE) team.

Check out the [Delite roadmap](/images/roadmaps/delite-roadmap.png) to see where we're going!

## Get started

## Contribute to Delite

Delite is an open-source, community-driven project that welcomes and values every and any contribution. That's including both technical and non-technical contributions.

Take a look at the [contributing page](https://github.com/delitesystems/delite-mono/contribute) to get started as a contributor!
